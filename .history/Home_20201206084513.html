WELCOME TO HDS

Welcome to HDS, the largest board Cut & Edge distributor in sub-Saharan Africa. We have 30 stores in 7 provinces of South Africa. Reliably supplied by a manufacturing & distribution hub, we are your one-stop solution for home décor. Our leaders visit international trade shows throughout the year to ensure we are continuously updating our beautiful in-house brands with the latest in fashion trends. 

Our products range includes (click to view)
High Gloss Boards , Melamine Boards , Post Form Tops , Moisture Resistant Boards , Laminate Sheets , Veneered Boards , Foil Boards , Shelving ,
Laser Cutting MDF , Stone Surfaces (quarts & granite) , Lamitex , Polymex , Plywood (Pine, Marine, Bending) , PVC Ceiling Panels & Profile Doors .

We will assist you from concept & design to selection of A-Grade boards from the widest range of the latest trends in the country. Once your cost-saving cutting list has been prepared to minimise wastage, we will professionally Cut & Edge your order with the latest technology to give you the perfect finish. Add your decorative touches from our wide range of hardware options and you will be ready to go! We have a network of trusted carpenters in all the areas in which we operate and can arrange delivery of your carefully packed order to your home!

Products & Services

We strive to be an exceptional, one-stop shopping experience. You can purchase your entire board Cut & Edge and hardware items from our extensive range of fashionable and affordable products. We supply bulk boards, single sheets or Cut & Edge of almost any wood-based material. From raw MDF, chipboard, veneered boards, foil boards, plywood, high gloss boards (both UV and acrylic), post form & stone tops and the widest range of melamine faced boards. We also supply your hardware for finishing touches – from quality hinges to chrome baskets, from bar handles to hanging rails, we have the complete range for all types of projects.
